Woobo Parent
App Redesign

Overview Research Ideation Iterations Deliverables Reflection

Overview

One of the projects I was in charge of during my time at Woobo was
redesigning the Woobo Parent app starting from user research to
creating final designs and collaboration with developers.

Research

I sent out a survey to Woobo’s current users and gathered results
from 340 parents. Then I selected 15 participants for follow up
phone/skype interview. Each interview is 30 minutes long. During the
interviews I asked questions such as “Can you describe a typical day
of your child’s routine with Woobo?“ and “When you first got Woobo
did you know about the daily routine feature?” in order to understand
how specific features such as the daily routine fit into our users’ lives
and how important such activities are for them.

From the surveys and interviews, I synthesized the following
insights about our Woobo parents:
1. Find relevant and valuable content to send to child’s toy
2. Voice messaging
3. Flexible and easily accessible routine activities
4. Easier purchase and subscription management
5. Get a clear sense of what Woobo can do for their family

Ideation

Following research and analysis, I collaborated with another
designer on the team to outline a new system architecture and
create wireframes for key feature improvements.

Iterations

I presented the new system architecture to our product manager
seeking feedback and adjusted the design according to specific
business goals that the company wants to achieve.
